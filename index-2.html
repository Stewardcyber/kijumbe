About KIJUMBE

The underserved traditional saving model

The Upatu traditional saving model is a community-based savings system commonly practiced in Tanzania and other parts of East Africa. It is similar to other informal savings and credit systems. The model is rooted in the principles of collective saving, mutual trust, and financial support within a group.

This model is highly effective in rural and low-income communities where access to formal financial services is limited. It promotes financial discipline, fosters community solidarity, and provides a safety net for members.

The KIJUMBE APP is a digital platform designed to modernize and enhance the traditional Upatu saving model. It leverages technology to make the process more efficient, transparent, and accessible. Here‚Äôs how it helps:
